How to make a Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich

  1. take two slices of bread from the bag
  2. take out your ingredients - 1 jar of peanut butter, 1 jar of jam
  3. get a couple of knives for spreading
  4. Using one knife for peanut butter and one for the jam, spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  5. spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  6. put the two pieces of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly meeting in the middle
